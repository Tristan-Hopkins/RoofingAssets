Owens Corning Oakridge® Shingles offer a harmonious blend of beauty and functionality, making them a reliable choice for enhancing your home's exterior.

  • Enhanced Aesthetic Appeal: Oakridge® Shingles feature a warm, inviting look with a variety of popular colors, providing a step up from traditional three-tab shingles.
  • Double Layer Nailing Zone: A full double layer in the nailing zone gives these shingles greater integrity and better holding power compared to shingles with wide single-layer nail zones. [Source]
  • Wind Resistance: Engineered to withstand winds up to 110 mph, and up to 130 mph with enhanced installation methods, offering robust protection against severe weather conditions.
  • Algae Resistance: Equipped with StreakGuard® Algae Resistance Protection, these shingles help prevent unsightly black streaks caused by algae growth.
  • Energy Efficiency: The Shasta White color option meets ENERGY STAR® ratings, reflecting solar energy and potentially reducing cooling costs. [Source]
