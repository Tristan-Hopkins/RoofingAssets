Malarkey Highlander® shingles are our most economical architectural shingles, designed to provide durable protection and enhance your home's curb appeal.

  • NEX® Polymer Modified Asphalt: These shingles are constructed using Malarkey's NEX® rubberized asphalt, offering improved flexibility, impact resistance, and granule adhesion, leading to a longer-lasting roof.
  • Impact Resistance: Highlander® shingles have a Class 3 impact resistance rating, providing reliable protection against hail and debris.
  • Wind Resistance: They come with a standard 110 mph limited wind warranty, which can be enhanced up to 130 mph when installed with specific Malarkey products.
  • Fire Resistance: These shingles hold a Class A fire rating, offering maximum protection against fire hazards.
  • Smog-Reducing Granules: Incorporates 3M™ Smog-Reducing Granules that help clean the air by converting smog gases into water-soluble ions, contributing to a healthier environment.

Warranty Information:

Malarkey Highlander® shingles come with a Limited Lifetime Warranty against manufacturing defects. For detailed warranty information, please visit Malarkey's official warranty page.
