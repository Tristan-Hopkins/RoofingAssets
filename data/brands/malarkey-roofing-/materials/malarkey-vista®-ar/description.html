Malarkey Vista® AR shingles are high-performance architectural shingles designed for durability, impact resistance, and long-term weather protection.

  • NEX® Polymer Modified Asphalt: Crafted with Malarkey's NEX® rubberized asphalt, these shingles offer enhanced flexibility, impact resistance, and granule adhesion.
  • Impact Resistance: Vista® AR shingles carry a Class 4 impact resistance rating, providing top-tier protection against hail and debris.
  • Wind Resistance: Standard 110 mph limited wind warranty, with enhancement options up to 130 mph when installed with Malarkey-approved products.
  • Algae Resistance: Equipped with 3M™ Scotchgard™ technology, these shingles include a 10-year algae warranty to prevent black streaks and discoloration.
  • Sustainable Materials: Incorporates upcycled rubber and plastic, diverting waste from landfills and contributing to environmental sustainability.
  • Smog-Reducing Granules: Features 3M™ Smog-Reducing Granules that convert airborne pollutants into water-soluble ions, improving air quality.
