Malarkey Windsor® Scotchgard™ shingles are premium designer shingles that emulate the traditional look of cedar shake roofs while offering advanced durability and protection.

  • NEX® Polymer Modified Asphalt: These shingles are crafted using Malarkey's NEX® polymer modified asphalt, enhancing flexibility, impact resistance, and granule adhesion for an extended roof lifespan.
  • Impact Resistance: Windsor® Scotchgard™ shingles boast a Class 4 impact resistance rating, the highest available, providing superior protection against hail and debris.
  • Wind Resistance: Designed to withstand winds up to 110 mph, with an enhanced wind warranty available for up to 140 mph.
  • Fire Resistance: Class A fire rating, offering maximum protection against fire hazards.
  • Scotchgard™ Protector: Incorporates 3M™ Scotchgard™ technology to prevent black streaks caused by algae, ensuring long-lasting roof aesthetics.
  • Smog-Reducing Granules: Features 3M™ Smog-Reducing Granules that help clean the air by converting smog gases into water-soluble ions, contributing to a healthier environment.
