IKO Crowne Slate™ - Designer Shingles with the Elegant Look of Natural Slate

IKO Crowne Slate™ shingles combine luxurious looks with premium quality, mimicking the high-end appeal of natural slate tiles while offering superior protection and durability for your home.

  • Authentic Slate Appearance: Precisely crafted to recreate the luxurious, sought-after look of natural slate tiles, adding a touch of elegance and sophistication to exclusive properties.
  • Class 4 Impact Resistance: Carries the highest impact resistance rating available, which may qualify homeowners for a reduction in their residential insurance premiums in eligible areas.
  • Heavy-Duty Construction: Premium asphalt and heavyweight design provide exceptional durability and cushion against the impact of hail and extreme weather conditions.
  • Generous Exposure: Features a 10-inch exposure that creates a stunning roofscape and enhances the visual appeal of upscale homes.
  • Superior Wind Resistance: When installed with IKO's High Wind Application and PROFORMAX Integrated Roofing Accessories, qualifies for limited wind coverage up to 130 mph (210 km/h).
  • Algae Resistance: Contains algae-resistant granules that help inhibit discoloration caused by blue-green algae, maintaining your roof's appearance over time.
  • Distinctive Color Blends: Available in elegant color options that complement a variety of architectural styles and enhance your home's curb appeal.
