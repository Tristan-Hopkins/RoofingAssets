IKO Cambridge® Cool Colors™ Plus - Energy-Efficient Architectural Shingles with Enhanced Solar Reflectivity

Cambridge® Cool Colors™ Plus shingles combine stylish aesthetics with advanced energy efficiency, featuring an exceptional Solar Reflectance Index (SRI) of 20 or greater that exceeds California's Title 24, Part 6 requirements by 25%.

  • Superior Solar Reflectivity: Embedded with infrared-reflective pigments in the surface granules that transmit less of the sun's solar energy into your attic, helping to keep your home cooler.
  • Energy Efficiency: Helps reduce your home's carbon footprint and potentially lower energy bills by maintaining cooler roof and attic temperatures, particularly valuable in warm climates.
  • California Title 24 Compliant: Specifically designed to meet and exceed California's energy code requirements, including the more stringent Los Angeles City and County cool roof reflectance standards.
  • COOL PLUS Technology: Offers sought-after dark color blends with high SRI ratings, allowing you to have the rich, dark colors you desire while still meeting energy efficiency standards.
  • High-Quality Construction: Features a resilient fiberglass mat core for strength and durability combined with exceptional FastLock® sealant that helps prevent shingle lift in high winds.
  • Algae Resistance: Contains built-in blue-green algae resistant granules to prevent unsightly streaking and discoloration over time.
  • True Square Advantage Size: Larger than most competitors' comparable products, allowing for faster installation and a more dramatic finished appearance.
  • Class 3 Impact Resistance: Offers enhanced protection against hail and may qualify for insurance premium discounts in some areas.

Color Options:

Available in three distinct categories to match your style preferences while meeting reflectivity requirements:

  • COOL PLUS: Premium dark color blends with SRI 20+ rating, including Weatherwood, Harvard Slate, Dual Brown, and Graphite Black.
  • COOL: Medium-toned blends with SRI 20+ rating, including Canyon Oak and Sand Dune.
  • Natural COOL: Light color blends with SRI 16+ rating for California Title 24 compliance, including Arctic White and Dual Grey (with additional algae resistance).
