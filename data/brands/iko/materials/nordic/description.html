IKO Nordic™ - High-Performance Shingles with Superior Impact Resistance

IKO Nordic™ shingles represent IKO's highest performing line, combining exceptional weather protection with striking aesthetics to deliver premium performance for weather-conscious homeowners.

  • Class 4 Impact Resistance: Reinforced with polymer-modified asphalt for the highest level of impact resistance available, potentially qualifying homeowners for insurance premium discounts in eligible areas.
  • Polymer-Modified Asphalt: Special SBS-modified asphalt formulation provides exceptional flexibility and tear resistance, acting as a shock absorber against hail impacts and severe weather conditions.
  • ArmourZone® Technology: Features a 1-1/4" reinforced nailing surface with a tear-resistant band that ensures exceptional fastening strength and prevents nail pull-through, even in high winds.
  • Superior Wind Resistance: Engineered to withstand winds up to 130 mph (210 km/h) with the use of only four nails when installed according to high wind application instructions.
  • True Square Advantage: Extra-large exposure provides full coverage with three bundles equaling exactly 100 square feet, reducing overall project costs and installation time.
  • FastLock® Sealant: Special adhesive strips create a strong bond between shingles, helping to resist water penetration and protect against wind uplift.
  • Advanced Color Blending Technology: Creates high-definition color blends that mimic the look of real stone tiles or wood shakes without the associated maintenance requirements.
  • Algae Resistance: Special granules embedded in the shingle surface help inhibit the growth of blue-green algae that can cause unsightly black streaks and discoloration.
