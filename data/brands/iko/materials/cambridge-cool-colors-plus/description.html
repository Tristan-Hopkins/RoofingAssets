IKO Cambridge® Cool Colors™ Plus - Energy-Efficient Architectural Shingles with Enhanced Solar Reflectivity

IKO Cambridge® Cool Colors™ Plus shingles combine elegant aesthetics with advanced solar reflective technology, helping homeowners meet energy efficiency requirements while enhancing curb appeal.

  • Superior Solar Reflectivity: Features an impressive Solar Reflectance Index (SRI) of 20 or greater, which can boost a roof's reflectivity by 25% over standard requirements, keeping your home cooler in warm weather.
  • Energy Efficiency: Specially designed infrared-reflective pigments embedded in the granules transmit less of the sun's energy into your attic, potentially reducing energy bills depending on location, climate, and HVAC systems.
  • Title 24 Compliant: Specifically engineered to meet California's Title 24, Part 6 cool roof requirements and exceed Los Angeles City and County cool roofing reflectance standards.
  • FastLock™ Sealant: Features specially designed adhesive strips that become extra-tacky in the sun to create a strong bond, providing protection against wind uplift, blow-off, and water penetration.
  • Fiberglass Core Strength: Built with a resilient fiberglass mat core for enhanced durability and long-term performance.
  • True Square Advantage: Larger-sized shingles provide greater coverage than many competitors' comparable products, reducing installation time and material costs.
  • Stylish Color Options: Available in a variety of attractive medium and darker-toned colors that maintain high reflectivity while complementing your home's exterior design.
