IKO Royal Estate™ - Premium Designer Shingles with Distinctive Slate-Like Appearance

IKO Royal Estate™ designer shingles offer the luxurious look of natural slate tiles with uncommonly beautiful, high-definition color blends and superior weather performance, all at a fraction of the cost of genuine slate.

  • Authentic Slate Appearance: Features random-cut contours and deep shadow bands that create a unique, bold look mimicking the luxurious appearance of natural slate tiles.
  • Heavy-Duty Construction: Engineered with added asphalt and premium materials for exceptional durability and long-lasting protection against the elements.
  • Class 3 Impact Resistance: Carries a UL 2218 Class 3 impact resistance rating against hail damage, which may qualify homeowners for insurance premium discounts in eligible areas.
  • High Wind Resistance: Designed to withstand winds up to 110 mph with standard installation, and up to 130 mph with high-wind application methods.
  • Superior Granule Technology: Special granules help inhibit the growth of blue-green algae that can cause discoloration and help defend against damaging ultraviolet rays.
  • FastLock™ Sealant: Features adhesive strips that become extra tacky when activated by the sun's heat, creating a strong bond that ensures maximum protection against wind uplift and blow-off.
  • Class A Fire Rating: Highest level of fire resistance available, providing enhanced protection for your home against external fire hazards.
