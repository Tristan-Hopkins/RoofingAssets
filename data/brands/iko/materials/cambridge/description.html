IKO Cambridge® - Classic Dimensional Shingles with Timeless Appeal

Cambridge® architectural shingles provide the beauty of natural wood shakes without the upkeep, combining high-quality asphalt construction with superior durability and a dimensional profile that enhances your home's curb appeal.

  • Wood-Shake Appearance: Features a random shake-look design with deep shadow bands that create the upscale appearance of natural wood shakes at an affordable cost.
  • True Square Advantage Sizing: Larger shingles than most competitor products, allowing for faster installation and creating greater visual impact with a designer look.
  • Class 3 Impact Resistance: Offers enhanced protection against hail damage, potentially qualifying homeowners for insurance premium discounts in certain regions.
  • Resilient Fiberglass Core: High-quality construction with a dimensionally stable fiberglass mat that's thoroughly impregnated with stabilized waterproofing bitumen for long-lasting protection.
  • FastLock® Sealant Technology: Special thermally activated sealant strips along the bottom edges become extra-tacky when heated by the sun, creating a strong bond to protect against wind uplift, blow-offs, and water penetration.
  • Blue-Green Algae Resistance: Contains special algae-inhibiting granules that help prevent unsightly black stains and streaking, keeping your roof looking fresh for years.
  • UV Protection: Colored ceramic granules surface both layers of the shingle to protect the asphalt from ultraviolet radiation, extending the life of your roof.
  • Environmentally Considerate: Shingles are produced and designed with consideration for environmental responsibility and sustainability.

Warranty Information:

IKO Cambridge® shingles come with a Limited Lifetime Warranty for as long as the original owner owns the home. They also include a limited wind warranty for winds up to 110 mph (upgradable to 130 mph with enhanced installation), and a 10-year algae resistance warranty. For detailed warranty information, please visit IKO's official warranty page.
