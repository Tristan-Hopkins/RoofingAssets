IKO Armourshake™ - Premium Designer Shingles with Authentic Wood Shake Aesthetics

Armourshake™ designer asphalt roofing shingles are IKO's ultimate offering in design, dimensional thickness, and heavyweight construction. These luxurious shingles perfectly mimic the look of hand-hewn cedar wood shakes while providing exceptional protection for your home.

  • Authentic Wood Shake Appearance: Features deep cuts, random patterns, and a contoured profile that creates the luxurious look of genuine wood shakes without the maintenance requirements.
  • Heavyweight Construction: Double-layer fiberglass mat design offers exceptional thickness and dimensional depth that enhances curb appeal and improves wind resistance.
  • Impact Resistance Rating: Carries a Class 3 Impact Resistance rating (Class 4 available in Shadow Black only), which may qualify homeowners for insurance premium discounts.
  • Superior Wind Protection: When installed with IKO PROFORMAX™ Integrated Roofing Accessories according to high wind application instructions, qualifies for limited wind coverage up to 130 mph (210 km/h).
  • Premium Sealant Technology: IKO FastLock® sealant strips are activated by the sun's heat, creating a powerful bond that helps prevent wind uplift and blow-offs.
  • Algae Resistance: Contains special algae-resistant granules that help inhibit the growth of blue-green algae, preventing unsightly black streaks and discoloration.
  • Class A Fire Rating: Offers the highest level of fire resistance, providing superior protection compared to natural wood shakes.
  • Distinctive Color Selection: Available in stunning color blends including Weathered Stone, Shadow Black, Greystone, Chalet Wood, and Western Redwood, allowing your home to blend beautifully with its natural surroundings.

Warranty Information:

IKO Armourshake™ shingles come with a Limited Lifetime Warranty for as long as the original owner owns the home. The warranty includes a 15-year Limited Wind Resistance coverage against blow-offs in winds up to 110 mph, with an available High Wind Limited Warranty upgrade to 130 mph.
