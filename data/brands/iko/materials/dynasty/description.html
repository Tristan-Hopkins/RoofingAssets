IKO Dynasty® - Performance Laminated Shingles with Advanced Weather Protection

IKO Dynasty® high-performance laminated shingles provide peace of mind for weather-conscious homeowners, combining superior protection with striking aesthetics to enhance your home's curb appeal.

  • ArmourZone® Technology: Features a 1-1/4" reinforced nailing surface with a tear-resistant band that ensures exceptional fastening strength and prevents nail pull-through, even in high winds.
  • Class 3 Impact Resistance: Carries a UL 2218 Class 3 impact resistance rating against hail damage, which may qualify homeowners for insurance premium discounts in eligible areas.
  • Superior Wind Resistance: Engineered to withstand winds up to 130 mph (210 km/h) with the use of only four nails when installed according to high wind application instructions.
  • True Square Advantage: Oversized shingles provide greater coverage, reducing the number of bundles required and lowering installation costs for your roofing project.
  • Fastlock™ Sealant: Special adhesive strips create a strong bond between shingles, helping to resist water penetration and protect against wind uplift.
  • Advanced Color Blending Technology: Creates distinctive high-definition color blends that enhance your home's exterior appearance and increase its resale value.
  • Class A Fire Rating: Highest level of fire resistance available, providing enhanced protection for your home against external fire hazards.
